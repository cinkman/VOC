What is Wastewater Treatment?

The spray painting process creates large amounts of contaminated water — paint mist captured by water curtain cabinets mixes with the water and becomes wastewater. This system cleans that water so it can be reused, and safely disposes of the paint sludge. It is a closed-loop system with zero wastewater discharge to drain.

Process Flow:

Spray Booths→ Recirculation Pool→ Air Flotation Machine→ Sludge Pool→ Filter Press→ Clean Water ♻ Returned

What is the RO Pure Water System?

Tap water contains dissolved minerals (calcium, magnesium, chlorine) that can affect spray paint quality and cause scale buildup in equipment. The Dual-Stage RO-2000 system removes these impurities to produce ultra-pure water at 2 tonnes/hour for use throughout the factory.

Process Flow:

Tap Water→ Multi-Media Filter→ Carbon Filter→ Cartridge Filter→ RO Stage 1→ RO Stage 2→ Pure Water 2T/H
