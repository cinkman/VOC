What is the Spray Painting Production Line?

The thermos cup flat-plate spray painting line is a fully automated production line for spray-painting stainless steel thermos cups and other cylindrical metal products. Cups move along a conveyor belt through 6 sequential stations — from dust removal to final cooling — each with specific equipment and process parameters. The line can handle cups from Ø50mm to Ø120mm in diameter.

Production Flow:

💨 Air Gun Dust Removal→ 🙌 Manual Loading→ 🌡️ Hot Air Pre-Heat→ 🎨 Reciprocating Spray→ 🔥 Hot Air Curing→ ❄️ Cooling & Unloading
